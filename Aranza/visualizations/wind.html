Latitude and Wind Speeds

Wind speeds appear chaotic without very recognizable patterns. They appear clustered near low values, but this could be due to the one outlier squashing the rest of the data together visually.

At a 95% confidence level, wind speeds in the Northern Hemisphere yielded statistically significant results, showing a moderate correlation with higher wind speeds at higher latitudes; this may be worth examining with the outlier removed, as the variation in speeds are within 3 m/s.
