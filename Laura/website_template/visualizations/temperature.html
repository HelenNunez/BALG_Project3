Latitude and Max Temperatures

These data are closely bunched and appear to make a rainbow-like shape, peaked between 0 and 20. As latitudes move away from the equator and towards the poles, max temperatures decrease.

Breaking these data out by Northern and Southern Hemispheres, the visual, linear trend in max temperatures is clear, supported by a linear regression with p-values that are so tiny I thought I might be making a mistake. As latitudes move away from the equator and towards the poles, max temperatures decrease.
